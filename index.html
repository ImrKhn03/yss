Welcome to Yash Sewa Samiti

Yash Sewa Samiti is an Indian non governmental organisation (NGO).It believes in selfless service of society to spread smiles for the upliftment & empowerment of the neglected and underprivileged sections of the society.
It focuses on the environmental & nature related aspects like Green Mission & Clean Mission to Save Earth
